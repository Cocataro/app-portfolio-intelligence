## Image Style Guide (For Design Team)

### General Direction
- **Mood:** Calm, warm, trustworthy. Not clinical, not cutesy.
- **Color palette:** Match each variant's existing theme colors (PCOS = warm plum/mauve, Migraine = cool blue, Chronic Pain = warm amber/terracotta, General = clean teal/sage).
- **Typography on overlays:** Clean sans-serif, large readable text. No cursive, no playful fonts.
- **Aspect ratio:** 2:3 (1000×1500px) — Pinterest standard.
- **Backgrounds:** Lifestyle flat lays or solid/gradient backgrounds. No stock-photo people. Mockup screens should show real tracker UI.

### What to Show
- Real dashboard screenshots in device mockups (not generic placeholders)
- Text overlays with one clear message per pin
- Warm, lived-in styling — blankets, tea, soft lighting — not sterile

### What to Avoid
- Stock photos of people
- Bright neon colors or harsh gradients
- Medical imagery (stethoscopes, pills, hospital settings)
- Before/after health claims
- The word "revolutionary," "game-changing," or "AI-powered" on any pin
