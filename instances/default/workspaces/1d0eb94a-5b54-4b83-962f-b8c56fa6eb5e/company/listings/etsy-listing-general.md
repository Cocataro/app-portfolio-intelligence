A: Only to download the file. After that, it works completely offline.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes. It's fully responsive. Works best on desktop or tablet for viewing charts, but daily logging works great on phone.

**Q: Can I share this with my doctor?**
A: Yes. You can show them your charts and patterns directly on your device, or print/screenshot the views you'd like to share.

**Q: Will I get updates?**
A: Yes. All future updates to the General Symptom Tracker are free. You'll receive updated files through Etsy.

A: No. This tracker works for anyone. Whether you have a diagnosed condition, are in the process of getting answers, or just want to understand your body better — it adapts to whatever you're tracking.
