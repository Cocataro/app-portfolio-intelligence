**FAQ**

**Q: Is my data really private?**
A: Yes. The file runs 100% in your browser. It makes zero network requests — no data ever leaves your device. You can verify this yourself using browser Developer Tools.

**Q: Do I need an internet connection?**
A: Only to download the file. After that, it works completely offline.

**Q: What happens if I clear my browser cache?**
A: Your data is stored in local storage, which can be cleared if you clear all browser data. The Quick Start Guide includes backup instructions. We recommend periodic backups using the built-in JSON export.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes. It's fully responsive. Works best on desktop or tablet for viewing the timeline and charts, but daily logging works great on phone.

**Q: When should I start using this?**
A: Anytime after you confirm your pregnancy. The timeline starts at week 1 and goes through week 40. Many people start in the first trimester, but you can begin at any point — your past weeks will simply show as unlogged.

**Q: Can I share my birth plan with my doctor or midwife?**
A: Yes. The birth plan builder generates a clean, printable summary you can bring to the hospital. You can also show your charts and patterns directly on your device.

**Q: Is this only for first-time moms?**
A: No. It works for any pregnancy. First-time moms especially appreciate having everything organized in one place, but the features are useful regardless of how many times you've been through this.

**Q: Will I get updates?**
A: Yes. All future updates to the Pregnancy Wellness Tracker are free. You'll receive updated files through Etsy.
