**FAQ**

**Q: Is my data really private?**
A: Yes. The file runs 100% in your browser. It makes zero network requests — no data ever leaves your device. You can verify this yourself using browser Developer Tools.

**Q: Do I need an internet connection?**
A: Only to download the file. After that, it works completely offline.

**Q: What conditions are the presets for?**
A: PCOS, Migraine, Chronic Pain, Fibromyalgia, Endometriosis, and General Wellness. Each preset comes with relevant habits pre-loaded, but you can add, edit, or remove any habit to match your routine.

**Q: What happens if I clear my browser cache?**
A: Your data is stored in local storage, which can be cleared if you clear all browser data. The Quick Start Guide includes backup instructions. We recommend periodic backups using the built-in JSON export.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes. It's fully responsive. Works best on desktop or tablet for viewing the heatmap and charts, but daily habit logging works great on phone.

**Q: What's the difference between this and a habit tracking app?**
A: Privacy and ownership. Apps store your data on their servers and charge monthly. This file lives on your device, works offline, costs once, and never sends data anywhere. Plus it's built specifically for health habits — not productivity streaks.

**Q: Can I share this with my doctor?**
A: Yes. You can show them your completion dashboard and charts directly on your device, export to CSV, or print the reports.

**Q: Will I get updates?**
A: Yes. All future updates to the Health Habit Tracker are free. You'll receive updated files through Etsy.
