**FAQ**

**Q: Is my data really private?**
A: Yes. The file runs 100% in your browser. It makes zero network requests — no data ever leaves your device. You can verify this yourself using browser Developer Tools.

**Q: Do I need an internet connection?**
A: Only to download the file. After that, it works completely offline.

**Q: Why is dark mode on by default?**
A: Migraines often come with light sensitivity (photophobia). Dark mode with carefully chosen muted colors is easier on your eyes during an attack. You can switch to light mode anytime.

**Q: What happens if I clear my browser cache?**
A: Your data is stored in local storage, which can be cleared if you clear all browser data. The Quick Start Guide includes backup instructions. We recommend periodic backups.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes. It's fully responsive. Works best on desktop or tablet for viewing charts, but daily logging works great on phone.

**Q: Can I track medication effectiveness?**
A: Yes. Each time you log a medication, you can rate it as "no help," "some relief," or "resolved." Over time, your Insights tab shows which medications work best for you.

**Q: Can I share this with my doctor?**
A: Yes. You can show them your charts, trigger patterns, and medication effectiveness data directly on your device, or print/screenshot the views you'd like to share.

**Q: Will I get updates?**
A: Yes. All future updates to the Migraine variant are free. You'll receive updated files through Etsy.

**Q: Can I use this for tension headaches too?**
A: Yes. The pain types, locations, and duration tracking work for any headache type. The migraine-specific features (aura tracking, phases, photophobia-safe design) are a bonus if you also get migraines.
