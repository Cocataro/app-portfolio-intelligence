**FAQ**

**Q: Is my data really private?**
A: Yes. The file runs 100% in your browser. It makes zero network requests — no data ever leaves your device. You can verify this yourself using browser Developer Tools.

**Q: Do I need an internet connection?**
A: Only to download the file. After that, it works completely offline.

**Q: What happens if I clear my browser cache?**
A: Your data is stored in local storage, which can be cleared if you clear all browser data. The Quick Start Guide includes backup instructions. We recommend periodic backups using the built-in JSON export.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes. It's fully responsive. Works best on desktop or tablet for viewing charts, but daily dose logging works great on phone.

**Q: Can I add my own medications?**
A: Yes. The tracker comes with 16 common medications and supplements pre-loaded, but you can add, edit, or remove any of them to match your exact routine.

**Q: Can I share this with my doctor or pharmacist?**
A: Yes. You can show them your adherence dashboard and charts directly on your device, export to CSV, or print the reports. Many users find the adherence percentage particularly useful for pharmacy visits.

**Q: Will I get updates?**
A: Yes. All future updates to the Medication & Supplement Tracker are free. You'll receive updated files through Etsy.

**Q: Is this just a fancy checklist?**
A: No. Unlike static checklists or PDF logs, this is an interactive dashboard that automatically calculates your adherence percentage, generates charts showing your patterns over time, and surfaces insights about side effects and triggers. Your data works for you.
