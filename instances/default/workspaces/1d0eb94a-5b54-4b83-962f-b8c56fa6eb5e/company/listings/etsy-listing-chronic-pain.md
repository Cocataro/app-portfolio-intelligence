**FAQ**

**Q: Is my data really private?**
A: Yes. The file runs 100% in your browser. It makes zero network requests — no data ever leaves your device. You can verify this yourself using browser Developer Tools.

**Q: Do I need an internet connection?**
A: Only to download the file. After that, it works completely offline.

**Q: What happens if I clear my browser cache?**
A: Your data is stored in local storage, which can be cleared if you clear all browser data. The Quick Start Guide includes backup instructions. We recommend periodic backups.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes. It's fully responsive. Works best on desktop or tablet for viewing charts, but daily logging works great on phone.

**Q: Can I share this with my doctor?**
A: Yes. You can show them your charts, pain patterns, and medication effectiveness directly on your device, or print/screenshot the views you'd like to share. The flare pattern data and medication tracking are especially useful for appointments.

**Q: Will I get updates?**
A: Yes. All future updates to the Chronic Pain variant are free. You'll receive updated files through Etsy.

**Q: Is this only for fibromyalgia?**
A: No. It works for any chronic pain condition — fibromyalgia, arthritis, neuropathy, back pain, autoimmune pain, or undiagnosed chronic pain. The symptoms, pain types, and body regions cover a wide range of chronic pain experiences.

**Q: Can I track whether my medications are working?**
A: Yes. Each medication entry has a 4-point effectiveness scale (No help / Some relief / Good relief / Resolved). Over time, the Insights tab shows which medications correlate with your better days.
